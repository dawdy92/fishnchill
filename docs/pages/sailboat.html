Sail & Dive with Dolphins

A week full of sun, sailing and epic dolphin encounters. Imagine yourself on a sailboat in the middle of the sea, only sharing it with a small group of likely minded people. This can be the magical equation of joy to anyone who considers himself/herself a thalassophile.

Itinerary

We depart from Hamata, Marsa Alam on the south of Egypts Red sear.  Exploring some of the most remote and exotic places in the region called Satayah Dolphin House.  One of the worlds best spots in the world to encounter more than 100+ dolphins), Hamata islands  and Abo Galawa reef

Details

What?

Out of the 6 days, we spend 3 days on the boat diving with dolphins. Once you get to dive with these amazing creatures for the first time, you'll never get tired of it. However, if we ever do feel like it and want to explore other things, there’s also the local reefs which often provide great dives. The diving in Marsa alam is said to be one of the best dive spots not only in world but in the world as well. Drop-offs, pinnacles.

Where?

Come and spend 6 days freediving with dolphins in Marsa Allam south of Egypt. Many travellers are drawn to Marsa Allam for the amazing combination of breathtakingly crystal clear waters and marine life. This will be the absolute trip of a lifetime!
