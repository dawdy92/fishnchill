Details

What’s included?

5-night full-board accommodation onboard Aziab sailing boat includes breakfast, lunch, dinner and refreshments

Daily 1 freediving session and 1 theory session

Equipment including: buoy, weights, lanyards

What’s not included?

Flight tickets, visa and optional insurance

Marine park entrance fees ($100)

Captain, skipper, guide and chef gratuities

Where are we going?

Marsa Allam lies on the southern Egyptian coast of the Red Sea. Many travellers are drawn to Marsa Allam because of the amazing combination of clear waters and diverse marine life.

What to expect?

Marine life encounters

Bottlenose Dolphins, Spinner Dolphins, Whale Sharks, Dugonog, Manta Rays, Various Reef Sharks and other diverse species.

Typical day

Start your morning with some stretching, watch the sunrise. Get suited and start your day with a dive across the reef.

Tell me more about the sailboat

You will be accommodated for 6 days and 6 nights on a luxury Beneteau 51-foot sailing yacht, with spacious 2-person cabins with private or shared bathrooms that includes a shower.
