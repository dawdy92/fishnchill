Details

What to expect?

Marine encounters

Bottlenose Dolphins, Spinner Dolphins, Whale Sharks, dugonog, manta rays, black tips sharks and an array of diverse fish and beatiful corals

Typical day

Start your morning with some stretching, sunrise watching. Get suited and start your day with a dive across the reef. 3 meals a day, coffee, tea, juices, soft drinks, fruits and snacks

Where are we going?

Marsa Allam lies on the southers Egyptian coast of the red sea. It is remote, pristine and abundant sea creatures. Many travellers are drawn to Marsa Allam for the amazing combination of breathtakingly warm and clear waters and marine life. Marine Life encounters Islands surrounded with crystal clear waters The best reefs with abundunt healthy corals and marine life Hamata islands,Sattayah reef,Abu Galawa reef,Malahy reef and Claudia reef

Tell me more about the sailboat

You will be accommodated for 7 days and 6 nights on a luxury Beneteau 51-foot-long sailing yacht, in spacious 2-person cabins with private or shared bathrooms.
