FAQs

  1. Where should I fly to?

    • Primary Airport: Marsa Alam Airport, Egypt
    • Upon request we can organize transportation from the airport to the boat

    2. If you prefer flying to an alternative airport please reach out to us

  2. Departure and Return Schedule:

    • Departure: Approximately 10 am on the first day
    • Return: Around 5 pm on the last day

  3. Can the Route Be Modified?

    • Please note that itinerary flexibility is essential for safety due to external factors such as weather, wind, sea conditions, technical issues, and traffic. Predetermined itineraries are not practical; trust your skipper for local, up-to-date advice tailored to your preferences. Refer to our Terms and Conditions for further details.

  4. Where can I get a visa?

    For most nationalities, obtaining an e-visa can be done online, please visit https://visa2egypt.gov.eg/ for more info.
